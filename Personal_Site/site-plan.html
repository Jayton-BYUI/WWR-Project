Overview

Purpose

Employers don't give a lot of effort when looking into a resume, or even give you much time to show all your accomplishments and projects. If only there was a place to put all of that information in one place. Well, there is! Have you ever thought about a personal portfolio website? I have years of experience in programming and web development and will be able to design a website fitted to you, pleasing to employers, where you can store projects, career overflows, contact information, and even a little bit about yourself. You will want to be a step above your competition and it is such an easy decision.

Audience

The target audience would be potential employers I am hoping will hire me to work for their company. These people are going to need a simple and an easy way to look at all my accomplishments and skills. The employers are going to be in either the business or tech field with experience in both. I will need to try and appeal to what they are looking for in an employee so, this website needs to be very professional.
